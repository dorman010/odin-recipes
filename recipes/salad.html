Middle-eastern Salad

This is a simple yet delicious salad that can be had at any time of the year or day!

Ingredients

  • 6 persian cucumbers
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/2 cup fresh chopped parsley (loosely packed)
  • 3 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp. lemon juice
  • kosher salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste (optional)

Preparations

  1. Slice cucumbers, lengthwise, into 8 spears. Now cut crosswise so that it is diced small. Place in a large bowl.
  2. Dice the tomato into pieces the same size as the cucumber and place in the bowl, also.
  3. Dice the red onion very finely, place in bowl.
  4. Finely chop the parsley, place in bowl.
  5. Drizzle all contents in the bowl with the olive oil, gently toss to coat. Additionally, pour in the lemon juice, toss once more. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
